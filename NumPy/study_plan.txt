NumPy Study Plan
================

1. ndarray (what it is and why it exists)
2. Array creation (array, zeros, ones, arange, linspace)
3. Attributes (shape, ndim, dtype, size, itemsize, nbytes)
4. Indexing and slicing
5. Boolean masking
6. Broadcasting
7. Vectorized operations
8. Reshaping (reshape, flatten, transpose)
9. Mathematical functions (sqrt, log, exp, sin, etc.)
10. Statistics (sum, mean, std, min, max)
11. Random module
12. Linear algebra
13. File I/O
